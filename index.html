🤩 Subscribe Notes

We try to be as transparent as possible about how we send these updates.

In a nutshell,

  • ConnectDome users are automatically notified through MailChimp for releases before 1.5 and releases after ProductHunt. You will be asked to opt-in or opt-out during registration whenever we re-open our platform to the public.
  • Releases between 1.5 and ProductHunt Launch will be notified to our users and wait list subscribers through ProductHunt Ship.
  • Subscribers are notified through Revue about our weekly logs.

Are you interested? Join the wait list at ConnectDome and get notified when we release the public version.
